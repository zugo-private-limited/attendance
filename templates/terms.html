Terms & Conditions and Code of Conduct

Effective Date: December 2025 | Last Updated: December 29, 2025

⚠️ IMPORTANT: By continuing employment with Zugo Private Limited, all employees acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agreed to comply with these Terms & Conditions. Violation of any rule will result in strict disciplinary action.

1. GENERAL CODE OF CONDUCT (APPLICABLE TO ALL)

  • All employees must act with professionalism, integrity, discipline, and accountability at all times.
  • Any form of misbehavior, indiscipline, dishonesty, manipulation, or disrespect towards colleagues, clients, or management will result in strict disciplinary action, including termination.
  • Employees must strictly follow company hierarchy and reporting structure.
  • Office discussions, documents, data, strategies, and client information are strictly confidential.
  • Mobile phones must be kept on silent during working hours.
  • Office gossip, rumors, politics, or negative discussions are strictly prohibited.
  • Discussing, spreading, questioning, speculating, or gossiping about the Managing Director's (MD's) personal life, family matters, finances, relationships, health, or any private affairs is STRICTLY PROHIBITED.
  • Any employee found talking, spreading rumors, or indulging in discussions about the MD's personal matters—inside or outside the office, online or offline—will face SEVERE DISCIPLINARY ACTION.
  • Such actions will be treated as GROSS MISCONDUCT and may lead to suspension, immediate termination, financial penalties, and legal consequences.
  • Personal work during office hours is NOT ALLOWED.
  • Employees must follow written instructions from management without deviation.

2. OFFICE TIMINGS & ATTENDANCE (STRICT)

Working Hours:

  • Office Working Hours: 9:30 AM – 7:30 PM (Monday to Friday, as per business requirement)
  • Lunch Break: 30 minutes only (Between 1:00 PM – 2:30 PM)

Attendance Rules (Non-Negotiable):

  • Employees must punch attendance before 9:30 AM sharp.
  • Late arrival beyond 9:35 AM will be marked as half-day or salary deduction.
  • Repeated late coming (3 times/month) will lead to warning + salary cut.
  • Leaving office before 7:30 PM without approval is strictly prohibited.
  • No Work From Home unless approved by Director/HR in writing.
  • Absence without prior approval will be treated as Leave Without Pay (LWP).

3. DRESS CODE POLICY (MANDATORY FORMAL)

For Men:

  • Formal shirt (Full sleeve preferred)
  • Formal trousers
  • Formal shoes (No sneakers/slippers)
  • Clean, professional grooming

For Women:

  • Formal salwar suit / formal saree / formal western wear
  • No casual wear, leggings, jeans, short tops

Strict Prohibitions:

  • Jeans, T-shirts, hoodies, ripped clothing
  • Casual footwear, sandals, slippers
  • Flashy accessories
  • Violation of dress code = Entry denied / salary deduction

4. WORK DISCIPLINE & PERFORMANCE RULES

  • Every employee must complete assigned work within deadlines.
  • No excuses for delay in delivery.
  • Daily task reporting is mandatory.
  • Poor performance for 2 consecutive months will result in termination.
  • All tasks must meet company quality standards.
  • Errors due to negligence will be penalized.
  • Unauthorized delegation of work is not allowed.

5. DEPARTMENT-SPECIFIC RULES

5.1 HR DEPARTMENT – STRICT RULES

  • HR must ensure 100% compliance with company policies.
  • Attendance, payroll, and employee records must be accurate and updated.
  • No favoritism or bias under any circumstances.
  • Employee complaints must be reported to management immediately.
  • Unauthorized hiring is strictly prohibited.
  • Confidential employee data must never be disclosed.
  • HR will be held responsible for discipline failures.

5.2 SALES DEPARTMENT – STRICT RULES

  • Daily sales reports are mandatory.
  • Targets must be met without excuses.
  • False commitments to clients are strictly prohibited.
  • Client follow-ups must be documented.
  • Any misrepresentation of services will result in termination.
  • Personal commissions outside company policy are illegal.
  • Sales staff must follow approved pricing only.

5.3 BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT MANAGER (BDM) – STRICT RULES

  • Responsible for revenue growth and new business acquisition.
  • Must submit weekly performance reports.
  • Must maintain professional client communication.
  • No unauthorized discounts or commitments.
  • Failure to meet KPIs for 2 months = review/termination.
  • Client data must remain confidential.

5.4 SOCIAL MEDIA MANAGER – STRICT RULES

  • Content must follow approved brand guidelines.
  • No posting without written approval.
  • Deadlines must be strictly met.
  • Any wrong post or error will be penalized.
  • Client accounts must not be misused.
  • Password sharing is strictly prohibited.

5.5 CREATIVE DEPARTMENT – STRICT RULES

  • All designs must meet professional standards.
  • Rework due to negligence is unacceptable.
  • Deadlines are non-negotiable.
  • Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
  • Company owns all creative assets.
  • Software licenses must be genuine.

5.6 IT & TECHNICAL TEAM – STRICT RULES

  • Code quality and security standards must be followed.
  • No unauthorized software installations.
  • Data backup and security is mandatory.
  • Deliberate errors or negligence will lead to termination.
  • Client systems must be handled responsibly.

6. DATA SECURITY & CONFIDENTIALITY

  • Company data must not be shared externally.
  • Unauthorized downloads/uploads are prohibited.
  • Any data breach will lead to legal action.
  • All intellectual property belongs to the company.
  • Employees must protect login credentials and access codes.

7. DISCIPLINARY ACTIONS (DETAILED & STRICT)

The company follows a Zero-Tolerance Disciplinary Policy. Any violation of company rules will invite strict action depending on the severity of misconduct.

Types of Disciplinary Actions:

  • Verbal Warning - Issued for minor first-time violations; recorded internally for future reference
  • Written Warning - Issued for repeated violations or moderate misconduct; becomes part of permanent employee record
  • Salary Deduction - For late coming, absenteeism, poor discipline, or negligence; amount decided by management
  • Performance Improvement Notice (PIN) - Issued for poor productivity or failure to meet KPIs; strict monitoring period applies
  • Suspension Without Pay - Applied for serious misconduct, indiscipline, or policy breach; duration decided by management
  • Immediate Termination - Applied in cases of gross misconduct including:
    • Gossiping or discussing MD's personal matters
    • Data breach or confidentiality violation
    • Fraud, misrepresentation, or dishonesty
    • Serious behavioral issues
  • Legal Action - Initiated in cases involving defamation, data theft, financial loss, or reputational damage

Management reserves absolute rights to decide the nature and extent of disciplinary action.

8. EXIT & TERMINATION RULES (DETAILED & NON-NEGOTIABLE)

Notice Period:

  • Minimum notice period: 30 days (unless otherwise stated in appointment letter)
  • Salary in lieu of notice may be required

Immediate Termination (Without Notice):

Company reserves the right to terminate employment without notice in cases of:

  • Gross misconduct
  • Breach of confidentiality
  • Indiscipline or insubordination
  • Gossiping or spreading rumors about MD or management
  • Reputational damage to company

Handover & Clearance:

  • All company assets must be returned (laptop, ID card, data, access credentials)
  • Proper work handover is mandatory

Final Settlement:

  • Full & Final settlement will be processed only after clearance from all departments
  • Any dues or damages will be deducted

Post-Exit Obligations:

Ex-employees are strictly prohibited from:

  • Speaking negatively about the company or management
  • Sharing internal information
  • Contacting company clients without authorization
  • Any violation after exit may lead to legal action

9. DECLARATION & ACKNOWLEDGMENT

All employees must acknowledge this policy document. Failure to comply with any rule will result in strict action. Management Decision is Final & Binding.
