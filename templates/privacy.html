Privacy Policy and Data Security

Effective Date: December 2025 | Last Updated: December 29, 2025

ðŸ”’ DATA PROTECTION: Zugo Private Limited is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of all employees, clients, and partners. This Privacy Policy explains how data is collected, used, and protected in compliance with Indian law.

1. INTRODUCTION

Zugo Private Limited ("Company") is committed to maintaining the confidentiality, integrity, and security of all personal data collected from employees, clients, contractors, and partners. This Privacy Policy sets forth our policies and practices regarding data collection, processing, storage, and protection.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Employee Personal Information:

  • Name, email, phone number, and contact address
  • Government identification documents (Aadhar, PAN, passport)
  • Date of birth, gender, and family information
  • Bank details for salary processing and financial transactions
  • Educational and professional qualifications
  • Employment history and performance records
  • Attendance and leave records
  • Medical/health insurance information (if applicable)

Client and Business Information:

  • Client contact details and company information
  • Project details, requirements, and deliverables
  • Communication records (emails, messages, calls)
  • Transaction and payment information

System and Usage Data:

  • System access logs and IP addresses
  • Login records and session information
  • File access and modification history
  • Network traffic and bandwidth usage
  • Security alerts and suspicious activity logs

3. PURPOSE OF DATA COLLECTION

We collect and process personal information for the following legitimate business purposes:

  • Employment Management: HR administration, payroll processing, tax filing, and benefits administration
  • Attendance & Leave Tracking: Monitoring office attendance, leave records, and compliance
  • Performance Management: Evaluating performance, setting objectives, and career development
  • Payroll & Compensation: Salary calculation, tax deductions, and financial settlements
  • Project Delivery: Client communication, project management, and service delivery
  • Security & Compliance: Maintaining office security, asset management, and legal compliance
  • Audit & Investigation: Internal audits, investigations, and disciplinary proceedings
  • Legal Obligations: Complying with statutory requirements under Indian law

4. DATA PROTECTION & SECURITY

Security Measures:

  • Restricted Access: Personal data is accessible only to authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored in secure databases with encryption where applicable
  • Password Protection: All systems use strong authentication and encryption protocols
  • Regular Backups: Data is regularly backed up to prevent loss or corruption
  • Access Controls: Role-based access control ensures only authorized users access sensitive information
  • Audit Trails: All access to sensitive data is logged and monitored

Employee Responsibilities:

  • Employees are responsible for protecting their login credentials and not sharing them
  • Must report any suspected data breach or security incident immediately to management
  • Must not download, store, or share confidential data without proper authorization
  • Must follow all data security protocols and best practices
  • Must properly dispose of confidential documents when no longer needed

5. CONFIDENTIALITY & NON-DISCLOSURE

  • All company data is strictly confidential and must not be disclosed to unauthorized persons
  • Employee personal information, client data, and business strategies are protected under confidentiality obligations
  • Confidentiality obligations continue even after employment termination or business relationship ends
  • Unauthorized disclosure of confidential information may result in:
    • Immediate termination of employment
    • Legal action for breach of confidence
    • Damages and penalties
    • Recovery of losses from the employee
  • Any data breach must be reported within 24 hours to prevent further damage

6. DATA SHARING POLICY

When Data Is Shared:

  • Data sharing requires explicit authorization from management or the individual
  • Personal data may be shared with authorized third parties including:
    • Banks and financial institutions (for payroll processing)
    • Tax authorities (for tax compliance)
    • Legal authorities (if required by law)
    • Insurance providers (for employee benefits)
    • Clients (for project-related purposes only)
  • Data is NOT shared with unauthorized third parties without written consent
  • All third-party recipients must maintain equal or higher security standards as the Company

7. DATA RETENTION PERIOD

  • Employee data is retained: During employment + 5 years after termination (for legal compliance)
  • Client project data is retained: Duration of project + 3 years after project completion
  • Attendance and payroll records are retained: For 7 years (statutory requirement in India)
  • After retention period expires, data is securely destroyed unless required by law

8. EMPLOYEE DATA RIGHTS

  • Access: Employees may request access to their personal data held by the Company
  • Correction: Employees may request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • No Sale of Data: The Company does not sell employee personal data to any third party
  • Grievance: Employees may file a data protection grievance with HR or the Managing Director

9. COMPLIANCE WITH INDIAN LAW

The Company complies with all applicable Indian laws and regulations, including:

  • Information Technology Act, 2000 - For data protection and cybersecurity
  • Indian Penal Code - For confidentiality and data breach offenses
  • Income Tax Act, 1961 - For financial data handling
  • Labour Laws and Employment Regulations - For employee data protection
  • Data Protection and Privacy Standards - Following best practices

10. DATA BREACH & INCIDENT RESPONSE

If a Data Breach Occurs:

  • Immediate action is taken to contain the breach and prevent further damage
  • Affected individuals are notified within 72 hours of discovering the breach
  • Management and legal authorities are informed as required by law
  • Investigation is conducted to determine the cause and prevent recurrence
  • Corrective measures are implemented to strengthen security

11. POLICY UPDATES & CHANGES

  • The Company may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in practices or legal requirements
  • Employees will be notified officially of any significant changes
  • Continued employment after notification constitutes acceptance of updated policies

12. CONTACT & GRIEVANCE

For any questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy:

  • Contact HR Department: Submit your request via email or in person
  • Contact Managing Director (MD): For escalated privacy concerns
  • Response Time: All requests will be addressed within 14 days

13. DECLARATION & ACKNOWLEDGMENT

By continuing employment with Zugo Private Limited, all employees acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agreed to comply with this Privacy Policy. Any violation of data protection rules may result in disciplinary action, termination, or legal consequences.
