Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

The Company is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of its employees, clients, and partners. This Privacy Policy explains how data is collected, used, and protected.

2. Information We Collect

  • Employee personal details (name, address, contact details, ID proofs)
  • Client information for project execution
  • System usage data for security and performance monitoring

3. Use of Information

  • For HR management and payroll processing
  • For project execution and client communication
  • For internal audits, security, and compliance

4. Data Protection

  • All data is stored securely with restricted access.
  • Employees are responsible for protecting login credentials and company data.

5. Data Sharing

  • Personal or company data will not be shared with third parties without authorization, except where required by law.

6. Employee Responsibility

  • Employees must handle all data ethically and lawfully.
  • Any data breach or suspected security issue must be reported immediately to management.

7. Legal Compliance

  • The Company follows applicable data protection and IT regulations under Indian law.

8. Policy Changes

  • This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.

9. Contact

For any questions regarding these policies, employees may contact HR or the Managing Director (MD).

By continuing employment with the Company, employees acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agreed to the Terms & Conditions and this Privacy Policy.
